About Us

An exclusive platform for all building professionals

Mistry.Store is India’s first organized platform that simplifies building material purchases for all building professionals like Architects, Interior Designers, Contractors, Builders, Infra Companies.

A comprehensive platform dealing in over 150+ brands across 20+ categories, catering to all building materials under one roof. At Mistry.Store, we experience success through the success of our partners. As your building material partner, we extend our best efforts and expertise to handle all your purchasing needs while you focus on your work.

Founded in 2022, Mistry.Store began by providing a streamlined platform to source a vast array of building materials at competitive prices. Now, we’re taking things a step further.
